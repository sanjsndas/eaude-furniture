Sales & Design Consultations

Are you an architect, interior designer, or a private client looking to furnish a luxury space? Our sales and design consultation team is ready to assist you. We offer personalized guidance to help you select the perfect pieces from our collections or to embark on a custom bespoke furniture project. Schedule a virtual or in-person consultation to discuss your vision, material preferences, and spatial requirements.

Our design consultants possess deep expertise in modern aesthetics and material science. They can provide:

  • Detailed product information and recommendations tailored to your project.
  • Guidance on material selection, finishes, and customization options for bespoke pieces.
  • Assistance with spatial planning and furniture layout.
  • Information on bulk orders and trade programs for professionals.
  • Virtual room visualization sessions to help you envision Eaude pieces in your space.

We believe that the right furniture can transform a space, and our consultants are dedicated to helping you achieve your design aspirations. From initial concept to final execution, we are your partners in creating truly exceptional interiors.
